## Why Sponsor?

Eleva.js is a labor of love — built and maintained by a solo developer passionate about pure JavaScript and exceptional developer experience. Unlike frameworks backed by large corporations, Eleva relies entirely on community support.

### Your Sponsorship Directly Funds

| Area | What It Enables |
|------|-----------------|
| **Development** | New features, performance optimizations, bug fixes, security updates |
| **Documentation** | Tutorials, guides, examples, API references, migration guides |
| **Community** | Faster issue responses, Discord support, Stack Overflow engagement |
| **Infrastructure** | Hosting, CI/CD, testing tools, domain costs |
| **Sustainability** | Long-term maintenance and project continuity |

### Why Eleva Needs Your Support

- **Zero dependencies** = Zero corporate backing. Community support is everything.
- **~2.5KB gzipped** of pure JavaScript serves thousands of developers worldwide
- **100% open source** — Your sponsorship keeps it free for everyone
- **Direct impact** — Every dollar goes straight to development, not overhead
- **Solo maintainer** — Your support directly enables continued dedication to this project

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How is sponsorship money used?

100% of sponsorship funds go directly toward:
1. Development time for new features and maintenance
2. Infrastructure costs (hosting, domains, CI/CD)
3. Tools and services for testing and documentation

### Can I sponsor anonymously?

Yes! Most platforms support anonymous sponsorship. Just select the anonymous option when signing up.

### Can I get an invoice?

Yes. Open Collective and GitHub Sponsors both provide invoices. For custom invoicing needs, [contact us](mailto:tarek.m.raafat@gmail.com).

### What if I need to cancel?

No problem. You can cancel anytime through your sponsorship platform. We're grateful for any support, however long.

### I represent a company. How do we proceed?

[Email us](mailto:tarek.m.raafat@gmail.com?subject=Eleva%20Corporate%20Sponsorship) with your company details and we'll set up a call to discuss your needs.
